How to upload a book list

  1. Download the book list as a Markdownfile (.md).
  2. Click 'Choose File' below.
  3. Choose your downloaded .md-File.
  4. After the upload the table is displayed here.
  5. Afterwards a certain number of lines can be rolled out.

Note: Make sure that your table is formatted correctly to achieve optimum results.
